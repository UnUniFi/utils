What is JPYX?

JPYX is a "stable coin" in Cosmos Network. The price of JPYX is nearly equal to that of JPY. The mechanism of stable coin is same to that of USDX in Kava blockchain.

What is Jasmine ($JSMN) ?

Jasmine ($JSMN) is a utility token for governance or commission used in JPYX blockchain.

Thank you, Kava team

Our project is a fork project of Kava. We want to contribute to Cosmos ecosystem and also Kava ecosystem.

Jasmine ($JSMN) token distribution

We absolutely do not do initial coin offerings or token sales. Every token will be distributed in return of your actions like being a validator.

Validators recruitment
